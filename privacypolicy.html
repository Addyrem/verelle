PRIVACY POLICY

At Verelle, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information as you engage with our women's fashion clothing brand under the Indian constitution.

Your trust is important to us, and we want you to understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services. We are dedicated to ensuring that your personal data is handled in a secure and responsible manner in accordance with the laws and regulations of the Indian constitution

To achieve this commitment, we have outlined the following Privacy Policy to explain the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. Please take a moment to review this policy to understand how your personal information is managed while using our services.

1. Personal Information

1.1 Types of Data: We collect personal information such as your name, address, email, and phone number.
1.2 Use: Personal data is used to process and fulfill orders, provide customer support, and offer personalized services.

2. Payment Information

Secure Transactions: We collect payment details, including credit card information, for secure transactions.
Note: We do not store credit card information, ensuring your financial data's safety.

3. Browsing Information

Data Collection: We gather information about your browsing activities on our website, including pages visited and your IP address.
Purpose: This data helps us improve our website, optimize your experience, and enhance our services.

4. Improvement

User Experience: Browsing information is utilized to understand how you use our website, allowing us to enhance your experience and optimize our services.

5. Information Shareing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to external parties. Your data is shared only with trusted partners who assist in delivering our services and are bound by confidentiality agreements.

6. Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology is used to encrypt sensitive data during transmission.

7. Your Rights

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Cookies

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also request that we stop processing your data or unsubscribe from our marketing communications. For more information about our cookie policy click here.

9. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be effective upon posting on our website, ensuring you stay informed about how your data is handled.
