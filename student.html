STUDENT DISCOUNT

Students: Get 15% Off

Verelle is happy to offer 15% off full-price and sale merchandise in stores and online to college students.

Simply register for an account with your .edu email address and a 15% off discount will automatically apply to your cart.
If you're going to a Verelle store, just present a valid student ID at the time of purchase.

The Student Discount offer is valid on full-price and sale merchandise in Verelle retail stores in India and on Verelle.com. Student ID must be presented in retail stores or the promotional code must be entered at checkout when shopping online. The discount is applied to your order total before taxes and shipping. This offer cannot be used towards the purchase of gift cards or third party brands. Offer is valid on one transaction only and is not valid on previously purchased merchandise. This offer has no cash value. Offer cannot be combined with any other offer except for the free Economy Ground shipping offer on orders of INR 1999 or more shipped within India. Qualifying orders for free Economy Ground shipping will receive free Economy Ground shipping with fifteen percent off offer code. Void where prohibited by law.
