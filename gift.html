Gift Cards

Give the Gift of Style

Looking for the perfect gift but unsure about the recipient's style preferences? Verelle Gift Cards are the ideal solution. They allow your loved ones to explore our collection and choose the fashion items that suit their unique tastes.

How it works?

  1. Choose a Gift Card: Select the gift card amount that fits your budget and the occasion. We offer a range of options to cater to different preferences.
  2. Personalize Your Gift: Add a personal touch by including a custom message. You can express your best wishes and let the recipient know how much you care.
  3. Deliver the Gift: You can choose to have the gift card delivered via email to the recipient directly or have a physical card shipped to their address.

Redeeming Your Gift Card

Recipients can redeem their Verelle Gift Card at checkout on our website. Here's how:

  1. Select Products: Shop our wide selection of clothing, accessories, and more.
  2. Add to Cart: Choose the items you love and add them to your shopping cart.
  3. Proceed to Checkout: When you're ready to complete your purchase, proceed to the checkout.
  4. Gift Card Redemption: In the payment section, enter the Gift Card code provided in your email or on the physical card.
  5. Apply Gift Card: Click "Apply" to deduct the gift card's value from your order total.
  6. Enjoy Your Purchase: Complete the checkout process, and your new fashion items will be on their way.

Gift Card Terms and Conditions

  • Verelle Gift Cards are non-refundable and cannot be redeemed for cash.
  • Gift Cards are valid for a specified period from the date of purchase.
  • If the order total exceeds the gift card amount, the remaining balance can be paid using other payment methods.
  • Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced, so please keep them safe.
